For arguments in .path()

      M/m - move to - (x,y)
      Z/z - close path - (none)
      L/l - line to - (x,y)
      H/h - horizontal line to - x
      V/v - vertocal line to - y
      C/c - curve to - (x1,y1,x2,y2,x,y)
      S/s - smooth curve to - (x2,y2,x,y)
      Q/q - quadratic curve to - (x1,y1,x,y)
      T/t - smooth quadratic curve to - (x,y)

      A/a - elliptical arc -
      R/r - Catmull rom curve to -

      M, capitalized is absolute, calculated from (0,0)

      m, small letter, relative, from last point where the pen left off

For arguments in transform()

        T/t - translate
        S/s - scale
        R/r - rotate in degrees
        M/m - Matrix
